About Demon Slayer

Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba follows Tanjiro Kamado, a kind-hearted boy who becomes a demon slayer after his family is slaughtered by demons. His sister Nezuko is turned into a demon, and Tanjiro seeks a cure while fighting powerful enemies.

Main Characters

Tanjiro Kamado

Water Breathing user with a strong sense of smell.

Nezuko Kamado

Demon with Blood Demon Art and strong will.

Zenitsu Agatsuma

Thunder Breathing user who fights while asleep.

Inosuke Hashibira

Beast Breathing user raised by boars.
